About NexCell

We don't teach entrepreneurship.
We do it.

NexCell is the premier Entrepreneurship Club of Rathinam Institute of Technology, Bangalore. We foster innovation, leadership, and the startup spirit among students — turning passionate learners into future founders through hackathons, workshops, founder talks, and live pitching arenas.

🎯

Real-World Problem Solving

Industry-relevant problem statements across diverse domains.

💡

Design-Driven Innovation

Structured design thinking to craft user-centric solutions.

🚀

Prototype-First Approach

Translate ideas into interactive prototypes using modern AI tooling.

🎤

Pitch Like a Founder

Communicate ideas clearly, confidently, and convincingly.

🌐

Entrepreneurial Mindset

Skills beyond academics — creativity, leadership, strategy.
